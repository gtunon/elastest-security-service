    How to use?

    • 1. Create a TJob that calls the ESS APIs for performing the scan
    • 2. Execute the created TJob through Elastest
    • 3. Wait for the TJob execution to finish
    • 4. Check the displayed alerts. The alerts shown in red are high-risk, the ones shown in orange are medium-risk and the ones shown in white are low-risk
